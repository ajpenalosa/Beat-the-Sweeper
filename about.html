About Us

Beat the Sweeper was conceived and designed by 3 UCLA Coding Bootcamp students in 2018. We hope this site can be a useful addition to our users lives. Our site works by saving a user's current location and comparing it to the dates, times, and locations of the cities posted street-sweeping routes. This information is stored securely and never shared with any outside parties.
